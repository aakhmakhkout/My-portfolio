Library

A well equipped and well managed library is the foundation of modern education system. Library has the same position in an educational institution as the heart in a body. It circulates the knowledge and information through body of the college. The College library consists of books of all specialties.

National and International journals are also available. Library is spacious with adequate seating arrangements. To get access to modern trends in the field of health care administration, internet facilities are also provided in the college. A qualified librarian is in charge of the library.

Laboratories

The college is spacious and has Lecture halls with most modern teaching equipments. Well-equipped AV Aids Lab, and Computer Lab, Nursing Foundation Lab, Anatomy Lab, Nutrition Lab, Community Health Lab, Maternal and Child Health Lab are provided in the college for practical training.
